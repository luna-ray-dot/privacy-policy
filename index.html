Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025

1. Introduction +

Welcome to our app. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in our app that uses Firebase, AI features, Gmail API, and other services.

2. Data Collection +

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile details via Firebase Auth or Gmail API.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, session logs, feature usage.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, IP address.
  • AI Interaction Data: Inputs and outputs for AI features improvement.
  • Cookies & Local Storage: To maintain sessions and preferences.

3. Use of Data +

  • Provide and personalize our services.
  • Authenticate users securely using Firebase Authentication.
  • Send notifications and emails via Gmail API.
  • Improve AI features and recommendations.
  • Analyze app usage and performance.

4. Firebase Services +

  • Firebase Auth: Secure login without sharing credentials.
  • Firestore / Realtime Database: Store user data, app state, and preferences.
  • Analytics: Track engagement for performance improvement.
  • Crashlytics: Monitor app crashes and errors to improve stability.

5. Gmail API +

  • Send app notifications or account-related emails.
  • Access email address with your permission for authentication.
  • We do not read or store your emails unnecessarily.

6. AI and Machine Learning +

AI features process your inputs to generate insights and recommendations. Data is anonymized where possible, and we adhere to strict privacy practices.

7. GDPR and CCPA Compliance +

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • You can opt-out of data processing for targeted personalization.
  • Data portability requests are honored within legal limits.

8. Security +

We implement advanced security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. We recommend protecting your device and credentials.

9. Data Sharing +

We do not sell or trade your data. Only trusted third-party services like Firebase and Gmail API access data as necessary to provide services.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy +

We may update this policy. Updates are published on this page, and the effective date is updated accordingly.
